Delete Account

Scintilla — Martolis sp. z o.o.

You can delete your Scintilla account directly from within the app at any time. Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Scintilla app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (accessible from the main menu).
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your email address and password
  • Your progress and synchronisation data stored on our servers (Supabase)
  • All account-related information held by Martolis sp. z o.o.

What Is Retained

Some data may be retained after account deletion:

  • Subscription records — purchase history related to your subscription is retained by RevenueCat in accordance with their privacy policy and applicable law.
  • Local data — data stored locally on your device (such as offline content) is not automatically removed. You can delete it by uninstalling the app.
