Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's store settings — Scintilla cannot cancel them on your behalf.

On iPhone / iPad (iOS):

  1. Open Settings → tap your name (Apple ID).
  2. Tap Subscriptions.
  3. Find Scintilla and tap Cancel Subscription.

On Android:

  1. Open Google Play → tap your profile icon.
  2. Go to Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  3. Find Scintilla and tap Cancel.

After cancelling, you retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID or Google account, not your device.

Open Scintilla on your new device, go to the subscription screen, and tap Restore Purchases. The app will automatically recognise your active subscription.

Make sure you're signed in with the same account used for the original purchase.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly within the app:

  1. Open Settings (icon in the top-right corner of the home screen).
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the action.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your data will be removed from our servers. Note that deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription — please cancel it separately in your device settings.

The app isn't working correctly — what should I do?

Please try the following steps:

  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Check that you have an active internet connection.
  • Make sure the app is updated to the latest version.
  • Restart your device.

If the issue persists, email us at support@martolis.com with your device model, operating system version, and a description of the problem.

My data isn't syncing between devices

Data sync requires an active internet connection and the same account signed in on both devices.

Please make sure you're using the same email address to log in on each device. If the problem continues, don't hesitate to contact us.
